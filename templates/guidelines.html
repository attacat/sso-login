Attacat360 guidelines

Welcome to the Attacat 360 process. This happens every March and September and is an opportunity for an open conversation with your line manager around:

  • Feedback you have received from your colleagues
  • Feedback from your line manager
  • Objectives for the coming months
  • Career and personal goals
  • Training requests
  • Issues you want to raise
  • Salary review

Star ratings

Please note the following general guidance around star interpretation:

  • Serious work required
  • Some work required
  • Meets the expected high standard of an Attacat
  • Exceeds the high standard
  • Is an absolute superstar
